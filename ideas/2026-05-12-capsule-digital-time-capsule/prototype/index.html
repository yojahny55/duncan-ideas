Use Cases
Moments worth preserving
👶

New Parents

Record a video every birthday. She gets all of them on her 18th — 18 videos from the parent she was at every age.

"Dear Sofia, today you turned 3 and you love dinosaurs. I hope you still do."
💍

Couples

Record how you feel right now. Open it on your 10th anniversary. Laugh, cry, remember who you were.

"Right now we're in our tiny apartment eating ramen. And I wouldn't trade it for anything."
🎓

Graduates

Write a letter to your future self. Open it in 5 years. See how far you've come — or how right you were.

"Dear 31-year-old me, I hope you finally learned to cook."
👴

Grandparents

Record stories, wisdom, and love. Your grandkids receive them when they're old enough to truly appreciate it.

"Your abuela used to make the best flan. Here's the recipe and the story behind it."
🎖️

Military Families

Send daily capsules during deployment. They open them all when you're home — a timeline of love across the distance.

"Day 47. Missing you. The dog is pretending he doesn't care but he does."
🤝

Collaborative

Everyone records a message. The birthday person opens one capsule with 20 messages from everyone who loves them.

"From all of us — happy 40th. We chipped in on something bigger than a gift."
