# LastLogin — Digital Estate Planner 💀

> **Decide what happens to your digital life after you're gone.**

## Problem

4.9 billion people have online accounts. Almost none have a plan for what happens to them when they die.

- **$7B+ in crypto** lost forever because nobody else has the keys
- **Subscriptions charge for months** after death because nobody cancels them
- **Cloud photos vanish** — iCloud/Google Photos accounts get deleted after inactivity
- **Social media becomes a ghost** — memorialized or hacked, rarely handled properly
- **Domain names expire** — businesses lose their web presence
- **Digital purchases die with you** — Kindle libraries, Steam games, iTunes movies

Google and Meta offer "legacy contacts" buried 8 clicks deep in settings. Password managers store credentials but don't handle access succession. Estate lawyers charge $2,000+ for digital asset clauses most people never complete.

**Nobody owns the "plan your digital death" position.**

## Target Users

- **45M+ US adults over 50** with significant digital footprints
- **5M+ crypto holders** with $10K+ in digital assets
- **3M+ small business owners** with domain/hosting/social accounts
- **Anyone with dependents** who'd need access to online accounts
- **Estate attorneys** looking for digital asset inventory tools

## Proposed Solution

**LastLogin** — a guided digital estate planner that helps you catalog, decide, and delegate your digital afterlife.

### Core Loop
1. **Catalog** — Connect accounts or manually add them (email, social, financial, crypto, subscriptions, cloud storage, domains)
2. **Decide** — For each account: memorialize, transfer, delete, or download & delete
3. **Delegate** — Assign digital heirs with tiered access (full, financial-only, photos-only)
4. **Dead Man's Switch** — Regular check-ins. Miss 2? Your designated verifier is contacted. They confirm? Your plan executes.

## Key Features

### 📋 Digital Asset Inventory
- Auto-scan email for account detections (like password managers, but for estate planning)
- Manual add with categories: Financial, Social, Media, Crypto, Business, Subscriptions, Utilities
- Value estimation for assets worth money (domains, crypto, digital purchases)

### 🎯 Per-Account Wishes
- **Memorialize** — Facebook memorial, Twitter archive download, Instagram preserved
- **Transfer** — Give someone else access (with instructions)
- **Delete** — Full account deletion with automated steps
- **Download & Delete** — Archive data first, then close
- Attach notes: "My Netflix password is X, cancel immediately"

### 👥 Digital Heirs
- Assign 1-5 digital heirs with different access tiers
- **Full access** — everything
- **Financial only** — bank, crypto, investment accounts
- **Sentimental** — photos, videos, social media, email
- **Business** — domains, hosting, professional accounts
- Heirs get a "digital key" they store separately

### ⏰ Dead Man's Switch
- Weekly/monthly check-in (your choice)
- Miss 1: gentle reminder
- Miss 2: contact designated verifier (trusted person who confirms)
- Verified: unlock instructions to heirs per your plan
- Grace period before execution (your chosen delay)

### 📦 Legacy Package
- Auto-generated "Digital Will" PDF (not legally binding, but comprehensive)
- Instruction letter for each heir
- Emergency access card (wallet-sized QR code)
- Annual review reminders

### 🔒 Security
- End-to-end encrypted vault
- Zero-knowledge — we can't see your passwords or wishes
- Biometric app lock
- No cloud dependency for critical data

## Technical Feasibility

- **Frontend:** React Native (mobile-first), shareable web pages for heirs
- **Backend:** Node.js + PostgreSQL, E2E encryption with user-held keys
- **Account detection:** Email API integration (Gmail/Outlook) with AI pattern matching
- **Dead man's switch:** Cron-based with push notification check-ins
- **Crypto:** Read-only wallet balance via public APIs (no private keys stored)
