# ShiftSnap 💵

**Instantly know what you earned after every shift**

*For hourly workers who never quite know their true take-home*

---

## Problem Statement

Millions of hourly workers—nurses, retail staff, servers, bartenders, hospitality workers—finish their shifts without knowing exactly what they earned. Between variable hours, overtime rates, shift differentials (nights, weekends), tips, and taxes, calculating real earnings is a mess.

From Twitter/X (@steve_lear_):
> "Nurses, retail workers, hospitality staff 👇 After a shift — how do you know what you actually earned? 🅐 Spreadsheet / notes 🅑 An app 🅒 I just trust payroll 🅓 I wish there was something simple"

The problem is clear:
- **Variable rates** — overtime, weekends, nights often pay different rates
- **Tips are unpredictable** — cash vs credit, tip pooling, tip-outs
- **Tax withholding confusion** — "I worked 45 hours but my check seems light"
- **Payroll errors go unnoticed** — who's checking the math?
- **No instant gratification** — you work today, get paid in 2 weeks, never see the connection

## Target Users

1. **Tipped workers** — Servers, bartenders, baristas, delivery drivers
2. **Healthcare shift workers** — Nurses, CNAs, EMTs with complex differentials
3. **Retail & hospitality** — Hourly workers with variable schedules
4. **Gig workers** — Multiple income streams to track
5. **Anyone hourly** — Who wants to see real-time earnings, not wait for payday

## Proposed Solution

**ShiftSnap** — Log your shift in 30 seconds, instantly see what you earned, track patterns over time.

### Core Value Proposition
- **Instant visibility** — Know what you earned before you leave work
- **Catch payroll errors** — Compare your records to your paycheck
- **Understand your real hourly rate** — Including tips and overtime
- **Tax estimates** — Stop being surprised by withholdings
- **Pattern insights** — Which shifts/days are most profitable?

## Key Features

### 1. Quick Shift Entry (30 seconds)
- One-tap clock in/out
- Auto-detect overtime based on your week
- Quick tip entry (cash + credit)
- Rate templates (day rate, night rate, weekend rate)

### 2. Instant Earnings Calculation
- Gross pay calculated immediately
- Estimated taxes withheld
- Net take-home estimate
- "Today you earned ~$X" confirmation

### 3. Multi-Rate Support
- Base hourly rate
- Overtime multiplier (1.5x, 2x)
- Shift differentials (night shift +$2, weekend +$3)
- Holiday rates
- On-call pay

### 4. Tip Tracking (for service workers)
- Cash tips
- Credit card tips
- Tip-outs (to bussers, bartenders, etc.)
- Tip pooling splits
- Running tip average per shift

### 5. Paycheck Verification
- Log each paycheck
- Compare actual vs expected
- Flag discrepancies instantly
- "You're owed $47" notifications

### 6. Analytics & Insights
- Weekly/monthly earnings dashboard
- Best-earning shifts (Friday nights > Tuesday lunch)
- Average hourly rate (including tips)
- Tax estimate running total
- Year-to-date earnings

### 7. Privacy-First
- All data stored locally by default
- Optional cloud backup (encrypted)
- Never shared with employers
- Your financial data, your control

## User Flow

```
1. SETUP (one time)
   └─ Enter hourly rates, job details, tax status

2. CLOCK IN
   └─ One tap → shift started (can auto-detect location)

3. CLOCK OUT  
   └─ One tap → enter tips if applicable

4. INSTANT RESULTS
   └─ "This shift: $187.45 (~$162 after taxes)"
   └─ "Today was 12% above your average!"

5. PAYDAY CHECK
   └─ Enter paycheck → compare to your records
   └─ "Matches ✅" or "Discrepancy: $23 missing"

6. INSIGHTS (over time)
   └─ See patterns, best shifts, running totals
```
