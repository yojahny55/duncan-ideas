How PromptGuard Works

🔍

Real-Time Detection

Detects PII, financial data, credentials, internal codenames, and custom entity patterns as you type — before the prompt leaves your browser.

✏️

Auto-Redaction

Sensitive data is replaced with smart placeholders like [CLIENT_NAME] or [DEAL_AMOUNT]. The AI still understands the context.

✅

One-Click Approval

See exactly what was redacted. Approve, edit, or override in one click. Your workflow stays fast.

📊

Audit Dashboard

Security teams get full visibility: who prompted what, what was caught, trend reports, and compliance-ready logs.

🔌

Works Everywhere

Browser extension for ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot. API gateway mode for internal AI tools. No app changes needed.

🏢

Custom Policies

Define your own sensitive patterns: project codenames, internal URLs, product names. Every company is different.
