## 🎯 Problem Statement

**The Cancel Time Bomb**

Everyone has old posts. That joke from 2014. That hot take from 2017. That retweet you don't remember. In today's culture, anything from your past can resurface and destroy:

- Job opportunities (HR googles everyone)
- Brand deals and sponsorships
- Political careers
- Public reputation
- Relationships

**The manual problem:**
- Scrolling through 10+ years of posts is impossible
- You don't remember what you posted
- Context changes — what was fine then might be problematic now
- Different platforms, different archives
- You can't predict what will be controversial tomorrow

**Real consequences:**
- Celebrities losing deals over decade-old tweets
- Job offers rescinded after background checks
- Journalists and politicians facing career-ending resurfaced posts
- Everyday people getting doxxed and harassed

### Secondary

## 💡 Proposed Solution

**PastProof** — An AI-powered social media archaeologist that:

1. **Imports your entire history** from major platforms
2. **Scans every post** through multiple cultural lenses
3. **Flags potential risks** with severity ratings
4. **Explains WHY** each post could be problematic
5. **Provides one-click deletion** or archival options

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. Multi-Platform Import
- Twitter/X full archive
- Facebook post history
- Instagram captions
- Reddit comment history
- TikTok (captions/comments)
- LinkedIn posts
- YouTube comments

### 2. AI Risk Analysis Engine
**Scans for:**
- Potentially offensive language (with context awareness)
- Cultural insensitivity (judged by TODAY's standards)
- Political statements that aged poorly
- Jokes that don't land in current climate
- Association with now-problematic figures
- Hot takes on events that resolved differently
- Dated slang that's now considered harmful

### 3. Severity Scoring
- 🟢 **Low Risk** — Might raise eyebrows
- 🟡 **Medium Risk** — Could be screenshot and quoted out of context
- 🟠 **High Risk** — Would require explanation if surfaced
- 🔴 **Critical Risk** — Career-threatening if found

### 4. Context Intelligence
- Understands sarcasm and irony (to a degree)
- Considers who you were replying to
- Accounts for the era's cultural context
- Distinguishes between your words and quoted content

### 5. Action Center
- **Quick Delete** — Remove from platform
- **Archive First** — Save locally before deletion
- **Mass Actions** — Bulk delete by risk level or date range
- **Watchlist** — Monitor for future cultural shifts

### 6. Continuous Monitoring
- Regular re-scans as cultural standards evolve
- Alerts when previously-safe content becomes risky
- New topic sensitivity updates

- Zero data retention after scan
- No selling/sharing of user history
- Option for fully offline scanning
- Clear data ownership

## 🤔 Challenges & Risks

1. **False positives** — Over-flagging benign content
2. **Context failures** — Sarcasm detection is hard
3. **Cultural subjectivity** — What's offensive varies by audience
4. **Platform API limits** — Twitter restricts deletion rates
5. **Legal considerations** — User owns their data
6. **Ethical debate** — Are we enabling bad actors to hide?
