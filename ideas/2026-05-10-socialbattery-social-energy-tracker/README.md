# SocialBattery — Social Energy Tracker

> Know your social limits *before* you hit them.

## The Problem

Introverts, neurodivergent people, and anyone with finite social energy constantly hit the same wall: they overcommit socially, crash hard, and need days to recover. There's no tool to help predict, track, and manage your "social battery" — that invisible energy reserve that drains around people and recharges in solitude.

- 50-74% of the population identifies as introverted (varies by study)
- 15-20% of people are highly sensitive (HSP)
- Neurodivergent adults (ADHD, autism) face social fatigue at 2-3x the rate
- Burnout from social overcommitment is the #1 complaint in therapy for introverts
- People constantly cancel plans last-minute because they didn't realize their battery was at 5%

**Current "solutions":** Nothing exists. People use vibes, gut feelings, or crash and burn. Some therapists recommend journaling — nobody does it.

## The Solution

SocialBattery is the first app purpose-built for tracking, predicting, and managing your social energy.

**How it works:**
1. **Log social events** — who, what, how long, vibe (energizing 🟢 / neutral 🟡 / draining 🔴)
2. **Battery drains in real-time** — watch your social battery deplete during events and recharge during solitude
3. **Predict tomorrow's energy** — AI forecasts your battery level based on your calendar
4. **Warn your people** — share a live battery status with close friends/partner ("I'm at 20%, rain check?")
5. **Learn your patterns** — "You recharge fastest with solo nature walks" / "Phone calls with Mom drain you 40% faster than average"

## Key Features

### Core
- **Battery Widget** — Glanceable gauge (0-100%) on home screen, watch, and status bar
- **Event Logger** — Quick-tap logging: "Coffee with Sarah (1hr, draining)" → battery drops 15%
- **Recharge Tracker** — Log solo time and see your battery refill rate
- **Calendar Sync** — Import Google/Apple Calendar, auto-estimate battery impact per event
- **Energy Forecast** — "Tomorrow you'll be at 30% by 3pm" — plan accordingly

### Social
- **Battery Share** — Share a live link or widget with partner/close friends showing your current level
- **Gentle Decline** — One-tap: "My social battery is low today 🪫 Can we reschedule?"
- **Partner Mode** — Linked accounts see each other's batteries, plan social vs rest days together

### Intelligence
- **Person-Specific Drain Rates** — "Dad calls drain you 3x faster than friend calls"
- **Activity Scores** — "You recharge 2x faster after walks than after TV"
- **Weekly Social Budget** — Set your ideal social hours/week, track against actual
- **Crash Prevention Alerts** — "You've had 6 social events this week. Your average crash happens at 7. Consider resting."

### Recovery
- **Recharge Prescriptions** — AI suggests optimal recovery activities based on your history
- **Post-Event Check-in** — 1-hour after events: "How do you feel?" (builds your personal model)
- **Social Hangover Mode** — Low battery mode: simplified UI, minimal interaction, just recovery tools

## Technical Approach

- **Mobile-first PWA** (works offline, installable)
- **Battery algorithm:** Weighted scoring — event type × duration × person × time-of-day × recent history
- **ML model:** Personalized drain/recharge rates learned from 2+ weeks of logging
- **Calendar sync:** Google Calendar API + Apple Calendar via CalDAV
- **Battery share:** Magic link with live status (no signup required for viewers)
- **Privacy-first:** All data on-device, optional cloud sync encrypted with user key
