# MoodBoard AI — Virtual Wedding Style Board

## Problem
Brides and wedding planners struggle to visualize how dresses, hair, makeup, colors, flowers, and décor will look *together* before committing. They save inspiration across Pinterest boards, Instagram saves, camera rolls, and group chats — but can't see it all composed as one cohesive look. The average US wedding costs $30,000+, and visual mismatches (dress clashes with bouquet, bridesmaid dresses fight the venue tone) lead to costly do-overs and stress.

## Target Users
- **Brides-to-be** (2.2M+ US weddings/year, $300B+ global industry)
- **Wedding planners** (150K+ in the US)
- **Bridesmaids** coordinating group looks
- **Grooms/partners** wanting input on styling

## Proposed Solution
A visual style board app purpose-built for wedding planning. Upload or snap photos of dresses, hair references, color swatches, floral arrangements, venue photos, makeup looks, shoes, accessories — MoodBoard AI arranges them into beautiful, shareable mood boards with AI-powered harmony analysis.

**"Do these work together?"** — get an instant AI color/cohesion check with suggestions.

## Key Features

### Core
- **Drag-and-drop mood boards** — collage your inspirations on infinite canvases
- **AI Harmony Score** — upload 2+ items, get a 0-100 "do these work together?" score with reasoning (color theory, formality level, season matching)
- **Color Palette Extractor** — AI pulls the dominant colors from any photo, builds your wedding palette automatically
- **Board Sections** — Bride, Bridesmaids, Groom, Venue, Flowers, Table Settings, Cake — organized by default
- **Share & Collaborate** — share board link with bridal party, planner, family; real-time comments and votes

### AI-Powered
- **Style Match** — "Will this veil work with this neckline?" AI gives yes/no + alternatives
- **Budget-aware suggestions** — set your budget, get lookalike alternatives at lower price points
- **Vendor color codes** — extract hex/RGB values from your palette for easy sharing with florists, printers, rental companies
- **Season/Venue awareness** — AI adjusts suggestions based on beach wedding vs cathedral vs barn

### Social
- **Before/After reveal** — compare your mood board to your actual wedding photos
- **Community boards** — browse real wedding style boards with item breakdowns
- **Bridal party voting** — bridesmaids vote on dress options directly in the app
