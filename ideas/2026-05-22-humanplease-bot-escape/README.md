# HumanPlease — Escape AI Chatbots, Reach Real Humans

## The Problem

Companies are replacing human customer service with AI chatbots — and consumers are *pissed*.

- **80% of retail fraud now uses AI** (Forter, May 2026)
- **Declining trust in AI customer service** — a 6,000-person study (AnswerConnect, Apr 2026) shows rising frustration and increased demand for real people
- **$3 trillion in global sales at risk** from poor AI customer experiences in 2026
- **Intercom launched an AI agent whose only job is managing another AI agent** (VentureBeat, May 2026) — the recursion is real
- Google, airlines, banks, insurance companies — everyone is forcing you through chatbot loops that end with "I understand your frustration" but never solve your problem

You know the drill: you have a real problem, the chatbot can't help, and there's no visible way to reach a human. You Google "[company] customer service phone number" and get a 2019 blog post. You say "agent" 47 times. You type "representative" and the bot says "I can help with that!" No. No you cannot.

## The Solution

**HumanPlease** — a community-powered directory of escape routes from AI customer service to real humans.

Search any company → get the fastest path to a human:
- 📞 Direct phone numbers (not the ones on the website — the *real* ones)
- 🔑 Magic phrases that actually trigger human transfer ("cancel my account", "billing dispute", "speak to a supervisor")
- 🖱️ Click paths through chatbot menus (Press 3 → say "agent" → wait 2 sec → press 0)
- ⏱️ Expected wait times and best times to call
- 📧 Executive escalation contacts (CEO office, VP customer experience)
- 🏛️ Regulatory complaint links (FCC, CFPB, state AG) when companies stonewall

## Key Features

### 🔍 Company Escape Directory
- Search 10,000+ companies
- Community-verified escape routes with success rates
- Last verified timestamps — stale routes get flagged
- "Currently working" vs "no longer works" voting

### ⚡ One-Tap Escape Scripts
- Step-by-step phone tree navigation
- Auto-dial sequences (app dials, pauses, presses buttons for you)
- Chat escape phrases copy-paste ready

### 👥 Community Intel
- Report what worked for you
- Upvote/downvote escape routes
- Wait time reports from recent callers
- "This company just went full bot" alerts

### 📊 Company AI Score
- A-F rating: how hard is it to reach a human?
- Trends over time (are they getting better or worse?)
- Comparison: "Company X makes you wait 12 min, Company Y answers in 2"

### 🚨 Escalation Toolkit
- Executive email finder (CEO, VP CX, VP Support)
- Pre-written complaint templates
- One-tap FCC/CFPB/state AG complaint filing
- Social media escalation scripts (Twitter support is still human!)

### 🤖 Bot Detection
- Calling a company? App listens and detects if you're talking to AI
- "You're talking to a bot" alert with recommended escape phrases
- Post-call: rate whether you got a human or AI (builds detection model)
