# DreamScape 🌙

**AI-Powered Dream Journal with Pattern Analysis**

> "Need a Letterboxd for my dreams but it's just a dream journal at the end of the day if I really think about it" - [@Partyanima3000](https://x.com/Partyanima3000/status/2025706442859176321)

## The Problem

People want to track their dreams but current options are:
- **Paper journals** → Hard to search, no analysis, easy to forget
- **Generic note apps** → No structure, no insights, buried among other notes
- **Basic dream apps** → Clunky, no AI analysis, no social features

The real frustrations from Twitter:
- "I keep a dream journal but can't remember if these things happened in separate dreams, all in one, or IRL"
- "I used to keep a dream journal. Imma start that back - I dream too much not to"
- "Tracking emotions with color... Reflect. Reset. Repeat."

Dreams are a window into the subconscious, but without proper tools to capture and analyze them, insights are lost.

## The Solution

**DreamScape** - The Letterboxd for your dreams. Log dreams quickly (voice or text), AI extracts themes and emotions, and over time reveals patterns you never noticed.

### Core Features

📱 **Quick Capture**
- Voice-to-text for fresh-from-sleep logging
- Template prompts: "What did you see? Who was there? How did you feel?"
- Works offline - sync when ready

🤖 **AI Dream Analysis**
- Automatic theme extraction (flying, water, being chased, etc.)
- Emotion tagging (anxiety, joy, confusion, adventure)
- Symbol identification with cultural/psychological context
- Lucidity scoring

📊 **Pattern Dashboard**
- Recurring symbols over time
- Dream frequency tracking
- Correlations with:
  - Sleep quality (integrates with Apple Health/Fitbit)
  - Moon phases
  - Life events you tag
  - Mood patterns

🌐 **Optional Social Layer**
- Share anonymized dreams (like Letterboxd reviews)
- "Dream of the Day" featured posts
- Compare your dream themes with friends
- Dream interpretation discussions

🔮 **Insights**
- "You dream about water 3x more before stressful weeks"
- "Your flying dreams correlate with exercise days"
- "Recurring character analysis: Your 'unknown pursuer' appeared 12 times this year"

## Technical Stack

- **Frontend**: React Native (iOS + Android)
- **Backend**: Supabase (auth, storage, realtime)
- **AI**: OpenAI Whisper (voice), GPT-4 (analysis), embeddings (similarity)
- **Integrations**: Apple HealthKit, Google Fit, Fitbit API
