# TradeInPro — Maximize Your Trade-In & Resale Value 💰

## The Problem

You just upgraded your phone. The old one's worth $200-400, but where do you get the most for it?

- Apple Trade In offers $220
- Swappa says you can sell for $310
- Gazelle offers $185
- Best Buy gives you a $250 gift card
- Your carrier has a promo for $300 credit

You spend 45 minutes opening tabs, checking each site, comparing gift cards vs cash vs credit, and then you still pick wrong. Meanwhile, your device depreciates ~3-5% per month.

**Americans lose $15-20B annually** by accepting suboptimal trade-in values. The average person leaves $80-150 on the table per device trade-in. Multiply by 2-3 devices per year (phones, laptops, tablets, watches, consoles, headphones) and that's $240-450/year in lost value.

## The Solution

**TradeInPro** — one scan, every value, maximum return.

Point your camera at any device (or search by name), instantly see trade-in/resale values across ALL platforms ranked by net payout. Gift cards, store credit, cash, carrier deals — normalized to actual dollar value so you can compare apples to apples.

## Key Features

### 🔍 Instant Device Identification
- Camera scan: point at device, AI identifies make/model/storage/color/condition
- Manual search with autocomplete from 50,000+ product database
- Import from your Apple/Google purchase history

### 📊 Multi-Platform Value Comparison
- **Trade-in programs:** Apple, Samsung, Google, Best Buy, Amazon, Gazelle, ItsWorthMore, Back Market
- **Marketplaces:** eBay (completed listings), Swappa, Mercari, Poshmark, Facebook Marketplace
- **Carrier deals:** AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon current promotions
- **Gift card normalization:** $250 Best Buy gift card ≠ $250 cash. Shows real value (typically 70-85%)
- **Net payout after fees:** eBay 13% + shipping, Swappa sale fee, marketplace commissions

### 📉 Depreciation Tracker
- Historical depreciation curve for your device
- "Sell by" recommendation: "Your iPhone 14 loses ~$12/month. Best time to sell: now."
- Future value projection (6 months out)
- Category benchmarks (phones depreciate faster than laptops)

### 🏷️ Smart Listing Generator
- Auto-generate optimized listings for each marketplace
- Condition assessment wizard with photos
- Competitive pricing based on current market
- One-tap cross-post to eBay, Swappa, Mercari, Facebook
- Title/description optimized for each platform's search algorithm

### 📱 Condition Wizard
- Guided photo checklist (screen, back, edges, camera)
- AI condition grading (Mint / Good / Fair / Poor)
- Condition affects value across all platforms in real-time
- "Be honest" mode — overgrading hurts your listing

### 🔔 Optimal Timing Alerts
- "iPhone 16 announcement expected Sept 10 — trade your 14 Pro NOW before values drop 15%"
- New product release impact predictions

### 💼 Portfolio View
- All your devices and their current trade-in values
