# PodSkip — Podcast Voice Filter 🎙️⏭️

**Skip to your favorite guest. Skip the co-hosts you don't care about. Listen on your terms.**

## The Problem

Multi-host and interview podcasts are booming, but most listeners only care about specific segments:
- You follow a guest on X and want to hear their 40-minute interview, not the 2 hours of co-host banter before and after
- A podcast has 3 hosts and you only vibe with 2 of them
- You want to skip ads, intros, and housekeeping segments to get straight to content

**Today's solution:** Manually scrubbing through the timeline, checking show notes for timestamps, or just giving up and not listening. Podcasts with 500+ episodes become unlistenable if you only want specific voices.

## The Solution

**PodSkip** uses AI speaker diarization to identify who's talking in any podcast episode. You pick the voices you want to hear, and PodSkip creates a custom cut — just their segments, seamlessly stitched together.

It's "edit controls" for podcasts. Like having a producer who knows what you want to hear.

## Target Users

- **Podcast power listeners** (10+ hours/week) who are selective about content
- **Guest hunters** who follow specific thought leaders across shows
- **Commute listeners** who have a fixed time window and want maximum value
- **Non-native speakers** who find multi-speaker conversations harder to follow
- **Accessibility** — hearing-impaired users who benefit from reduced speaker count

## How It Works

1. **Paste or search** for any podcast episode
2. **AI analyzes** the audio using speaker diarization (identifies unique voices)
3. **Preview speakers** — hear a sample of each detected voice, label them (Host, Guest A, Guest B)
4. **Pick your mix** — toggle which speakers you want
5. **Listen** — PodSkip plays a seamless cut with only your selected speakers

## Competition

| Product | What it does | Gap |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| Apple Podcasts | Basic playback, chapters | No speaker filtering |
| Spotify | Playback, transcripts | No voice-based filtering |
| Overcast | Smart Speed, skip silence | Global speed, not per-speaker |
| Snipd | AI highlights, clip sharing | Highlights, not filtering |
| Descript | Audio editing (creator tool) | Creator-focused, not listener UX |

**Nobody owns the "listen to only who you want" position.** Descript has the tech but targets creators. Snipd clips moments but doesn't filter speakers. PodSkip is the consumer-first voice filter.

2. **Podcast transcripts** are now standard (Apple, Spotify auto-generate them)
