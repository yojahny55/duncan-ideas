How It Works

AI speaker diarization meets consumer UX.

🔍

1. Find an Episode

Search any podcast or paste a link. PodSkip works with any show on any platform.

🧠

2. AI Detects Speakers

Speaker diarization identifies every voice in the episode. Label them — host, guest, whatever.

🎛️

3. Pick Your Mix

Toggle which speakers you want. See a visual timeline of who talks when.

▶️

4. Listen Seamlessly

PodSkip stitches selected segments together. Smooth transitions, no jumps or gaps.

🔔

5. Follow Guests

Subscribe to a person. Get notified when they appear on ANY podcast.

✂️

6. Export Clips

Save and share 30-60 second clips of specific speakers. Perfect for social.
