## Problem Statement

The average person has **12+ active subscriptions** totaling $200-400/month. Most people:
- Don't know their true monthly/annual subscription spend
- Keep paying for services they haven't used in 30+ days
- Have overlapping subscriptions (Spotify + Apple Music, Netflix + Max + Disney+)
- Get blindsided by annual renewals they forgot about

**Existing tools** (Rocket Money, Truebill) focus on cancellation as a service — they take a cut or charge you. Nobody gives you a simple, honest **health score** for your subscription life.

## Solution

**SubLeak** — A subscription leak detector with a single killer metric: your **Leak Score (0-100)**.

- **0** = Airtight. Every dollar spent is on something you actively use.
- **100** = Hemorrhaging. You're burning money on forgotten, unused, and overlapping services.

### Core Features
1. **Leak Score** — Composite score based on: unused services, overlap detection, renewal shock risk, and total spend vs. benchmarks
2. **Overlap Radar** — Detects functionally identical subscriptions (music, streaming, cloud storage, note-taking)
3. **Renewal Alerts** — 7-day warning before any annual subscription renews, with one-tap cancel link
4. **Spend Timeline** — Visual of how your subscriptions grew over time (the "boiling frog" view)
5. **Benchmark Comparison** — "People like you spend $X. You spend $Y."

### Privacy-First
- No bank login required. Manual entry or CSV upload from your bank.
- Data stays on device (local-first with optional sync).
